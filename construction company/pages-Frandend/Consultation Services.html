Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our consultation services

What is included in your initial consultation?

Our initial consultation includes a comprehensive project assessment, preliminary budget estimation, timeline projection, and identification of potential challenges and opportunities. We'll also discuss your goals, requirements, and vision for the project.

How long does the consultation process typically take?

The duration depends on the project's complexity. A basic consultation can be completed in 1-2 weeks, while more complex projects may require 3-4 weeks for thorough analysis and planning.

Do you assist with obtaining permits and approvals?

Yes, we provide guidance through the entire permitting process. Our team is familiar with Dubai's regulations and can help navigate the approval process efficiently, potentially saving you time and avoiding costly delays.

Can you help with cost-saving strategies?

Absolutely. Our value engineering approach identifies opportunities to reduce costs without compromising quality. We analyze materials, methods, and design alternatives to maximize your return on investment.

Do you provide ongoing consultation during construction?

Yes, we offer ongoing consultation services to ensure your project stays on track. We can provide site visits, progress evaluations, and problem-solving support throughout the construction phase.
